University of California, Riverside Housing Brochure

One of the biggest decisions a college student must make is where they will live for their undergraduate and graduate careers. The goal of this recruitment campaign is to educate students about their living options while making the decision process hassle free. Using a brochure that features fresh and colorful graphics, a QR campaign that encourages user interaction with printed material, and an online 360 Virtual Tour of each housing type, I was able to create the ideal atmosphere for exhibiting the University's many housing options.
