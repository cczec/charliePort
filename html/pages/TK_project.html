The Teaching Kitchen @ Stanford

The Teaching Kitchen @ Stanford is an educational cooking program that is committed to promoting a healthy lifestyle for students and faculty on campus. The classes allow the Stanford community the opportunity to learn the fundamentals of cooking as well as the importance of using sustainable, local and organic ingredients. Inspiration for the campaign and environmental aesthetics for the Teaching Kitchen were influenced by the partnership with food education advocate Jaime Oliver. The brand presents a sense of growth and community using fluid and bold typography in conjunction with vibrant expressive photography.
