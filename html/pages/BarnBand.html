Concert Series at The Barn

The Barn is a concert venue that has been a stepping stone stage for bands like No Doubt and Rage Against The Machine. With a rich history of diverse musical artists, The Barn needed art that was always changing with each personality that stepped on stage. Each season six to seven band posters were created with one all encompassing lineup that also had its unique take on the seasons touring groups.
