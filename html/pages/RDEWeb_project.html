Stanford R&DE Webpage

Stanford R&DE (Residential & Dining Enterprises) is composed of four main departments: housing, dining, hospitality and conferences. The challenge for this website was to create layouts that unify these units under one R&DE design while keeping some individuality and flexibility for each department page. The solution was to have a design that is minimalistic, but photographically impactful. The layouts and templates for users are flexible and customizable, while still keeping to R&DE and Stanford's brand standards.
